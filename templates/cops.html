About Us

Club of Programmers (IIT BHU) is a club that encourages and organizes several activities related to the various aspects of computer science like competitive coding, app development, machine learning, artificial intelligence, cryptography, data science, etc.

Vision

To organize workshops on the vast magnitude of domains that comprise computer science and mathematics like machine learning, network security, app development to name a few.

To increase participation across all branches and especially among girls.

To organize more competitions

To bring all the tech end activities in college under its umbrella.
