About Us

The Aeromodelling Club at IIT (BHU), Varanasi was primarily started three years ago. The club has been running successfully since then and has expanded exponentially with each progressing year.

So far, many new, innovative and exciting models have been constructed and have seen successful flights, some of which include, IC-RC Plane, Autonomous Quadcopter, Tricopter, Hovercraft, and much more. That excludes the regular, yet critical models: Water Rocket, Glider, and RC Plane.

The club readily captures the attention of the newcomers who are enthusiastically receptive to the activities. Moreover, the club also organizes various welcome events such as Water Rocket Pro and Glider Competition, which witness mass participation from first-year students. The club also boasts about conducting four major events in the annual technical festival of the college (Technex):

Momentum (Water Rocket)

La Trajectoire (RC Electric Plane)

De Aeroglisseur (Hovercraft)

Drone-Tech (Multirotor)

An Airshow is organized each year by the club in the memory of Prof.

Veer Bhadra Mishra in which both external and internal flyers present their best models and flying skills.

Vision

In the previous years, the members of the club have gained experience in the field and also have worked on various basic models. They have also participated in various national level inter-college competitions.

However, it is time to take it to the next step. We will aim to improve drastically and bring out improvements in the technical knowledge as well as further polish up the flying skills. To meet international aeromodelling standards, it is required to ensure that the models which the students work on are not just some â€˜make and do' ones, but rather designed correctly, studied and then brought out in results that fly with perfection.

We will also be trying to introduce some new types of models in the club like helicopters in the upcoming session. Our participation so far has been woefully limited to national level competitions. Therefore, we intend to participate on an international scale sending out our most sophisticated equipment and the best amongst the flyers.

Apart from this, one of the most fundamental requirement of the club is the active participation of the first year students. To ensure this, we plan to work further on our workshop presentations, making them more practical, and therefore more enjoyable.

Achievements

  • Organized spectacular Airshows for three consecutive years: 2014, 2015 and 2016.
  • Participated and won at technical festivals of IIT Bombay, IIT Roorkee, and IIT Kanpur.
  • Successfully completed a project on Solar Plane.
  • Successfully built and taken flights of Tricopter, and Autonomous quadcopter.
  • Also completed works on racing quadcopter fully equipped with FPV System.
  • Designed, built and taken flights of the first ever IC RC Plane in the college.

Year-wise Activities

  • At the beginning of the session, Workshops are conducted on basic aeromodelling: Water Rocket, Glider, and RC Plane.
  • The club organises pre-technex events: Water Rocket Pro, Glider Fabrication.
  • Mentoring of students on fabrication and flying/operating of electric RC planes, Quadcopter, Hovercraft.
  • An official Contingent of the club participates in inter IIT technical fests: Techfest, Techkriti, and Cognizance.
  • The club organized four events in the Institute's techno-management fest, Technex, which witnessed active participation from external and internal teams.
  • The Club also organized its annual Airshow in memory of Prof. Veer Bhadra Mishra.
