About Us

The technical and rural outreach club of IIT BHU holds the work of reaching out to the people and helping them with technical ideas, bringing out ways and means to develop skills in them and develop ideas for environmental welfare.

The club is in its nascent stage and needs to be taken forward throughvarious means and activities for it to achieve its internal essence. A lot needs to be done in the future for achieving what the club is meant for and surely things will be done in this direction.

Vision

We are planning for a two tier system wherein the two wings will be the technical and the economical wing.

The technical wing will deal with projects and models pertaining to energyconservation, waste management, reduction in pollution and the case studies related to these topics.

The economic wing will be responsible with projects like rural entrepreneurship, ideas for stand up and start-up India.

We are also planning to conduct more workshops like the one hosted byMr Manish Jain on the topic ‘Gleam in the Eyes: Science stories and Toys’.

Achievements

  • The paper bag campaign organised by the club saw a huge success. It witnessed huge participation and a whole lot of paper bags were prepared under the scheme.
  • Also the Best out of Waste event under Technex or the Recycle fair came out with several ideas wherein the participants prepared models taking the waste items as a base and converting them into a useful product.
