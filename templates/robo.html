About Us

The Robotics club of IIT (BHU) Varanasi is one of the most active clubs in the entire college. It has been a successful club since many years and is growing year by year. It organizes year-long activities for the students especially for the Fresher's. The club promotes friendly learning of many of the difficult tasks & topics and encourages the young minds to develop the robots that would be helpful to mankind in forthcoming future.

Vision

The club believes in promoting technology and innovation in the college. It undertakes projects and mentor students in fields like manual robotics, autonomous robotics, embedded systems etc. The Robotics club promote robotics in the college not only as a hobby but also as a career option. We try to make the students aware about what is happening in the world of Robotics around the globe. We hope to grow as a team and develop technologies that would take the club to different heights.

Achievements

  • 4 teams made it to the 2nd round of E-Yantra Robotics Competiton.

  • Amol Kankane reached the top 8 of International Robotics Challlenge at Techkriti, IIT Kanpur

  • Himanshu Gangawar and Aditya Garg secured 2nd position in the GE Edison Challenge.

  • Projects like Home Automation and Posture Correction device were completed under the mentorship of Robotics Club which secured 3rd position in Technex'15 and 16 respectively.

  • Om Kumar Sahoo and Soumyadeep Das secured first position in Block Warriors at Techhfest'14, IIT Bombay.

Year Wise Activities

  • Conducts workshops on topics ranging from autonomous robotics, wireless communication to Hydraulics, Manual Robotics and Combat Robotics.

  • Conducts a semi-autonomous event for the first year students of IIT (BHU). Last year it was named RoboTron where Robots played soccer.4

  • Succesfully organises and manages the Robonex Section of Technex, the Techno- Management Fest of IIT (BHU)

  • Organises event-specefic workshops and provides mentors for facilitating participation in Technex.

  • Robots fabricated by the members of the Club are displayed during Technex Exhibition and Centennial Celebrations

  • Members indulge in Year Long Projects under the aegis of the Club. Last Year, the club took up Robotic Hand and Autonomous Underwater Vehicle.

  • Represents the college in Robotic events at various Technical Fests and Competitions.
