  • Organized Clean Campus Drives which have received good participation from the students.

  • Shared a message to “switch off the lights when not in use” on every door of each hostel of our institute.

  • Posters and stickers on water coolers and wash rooms to reduce water wastage.

  • Flexes in hostel mess to spread awareness about food wastage.

  • Ban the use of disposable/single use plastic inside campus.
